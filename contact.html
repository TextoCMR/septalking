Talk to the Train!

SEPTAlking is a multi-modal app that lets you use the most natural interface of all - your voice - to get information on SEPTA regional rail departures and arrivals. Oh, and it's open source.

About

SEPTAlking was created by Mark Headd at the very first Apps for SEPTA Hackathon. Mark is a civic coder and open data advocate who works as the Chief Data Officer for the City of Philadelphia.

Mark has built telephony, speech recognition and communication applications for over a decade and is proficient in a variety of programming languages, database technologies and telephony platforms. He is a Code for America alumni that occasionally blogs at civic.io
