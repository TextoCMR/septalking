SEPTAlking by mheadd

A telephone / SMS / IM app that lets you find departure times for SEPTA trains.

SEPTAlking is a telephone based speech-recognition application that lets you use your voice to find train departure times.

The application uses the SEPTA "Next to arrive" API, and provides information on the train arrivals and departures from SEPTA regional rail stations.

Users can interact with SEPTAlking using the following methods:

Voice & SMS: (215) 987-5418
Skype Voice: +990009369996146146
SIP Voice: sip:9996146146@sip.tropo.com
Jabber IM: septalking@tropo.im
